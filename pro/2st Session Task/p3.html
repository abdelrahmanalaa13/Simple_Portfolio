About Eng Break

Eng Break Is A Student Activity In Cairo University, Faculty Of Engineering, Which Aims To Fill The Gap Between Academic Life And Profession Life.
3rd best student activity in Faculty of Engineering Cairo University 2013.
Providing creative projects focusing on values of pioneers generation.
Fundraising committee is responsible for: -

-Getting enough fund by sponsorship to cover all events budget
-Providing printing sponsors
-Providing academic sponsors for all workshops
-Maintaining good relations with sponsors
-Proving Eng Break with fund by sponsorship.
-Market research before any meeting with company.
-Helping PR sharing and stabilizing Eng Break image among companies and sponsors.
-Provide Eng Break with database for the contacts of the companies
